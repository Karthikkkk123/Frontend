Topic 3: Organic Chemistry

Organic chemistry is a subdiscipline within chemistry involving the scientific study of the structure, properties, and reactions of organic compounds and organic materials.

Functional Groups

Functional groups are specific groups of atoms or bonds within molecules that are responsible for the characteristic chemical reactions of those molecules.
